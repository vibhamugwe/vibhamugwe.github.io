About

Vibha G Mugwe is a second-year CSE undergrad student of Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering. She hails from Mysuru and has completed her primary and secondary education in Vidyavardhaka Sangha school and her pre-uni education in Base PU college, both of which are situated in Mysuru. She has been a finalist in the International Level of MaRRS Spelling Bee conducted at New Delhi in 2009. She has also represented her school and won the first prize at the Regional Level CBSE Science exhibition which was held at Army Public School, Bengaluru in 2014.

Vibha loves to sing as well as do zentangle art in her free time. She thoroughly enjoys reading novels and has just gotten into reading self-help books. She loves experimenting new cuisines and enjoys preparing them as well!
